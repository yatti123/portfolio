CSM TRANSFORMATION

Telecom Domain

CSM describes the design of Rules Framework at high level. This rules frame work is part of existing CSM (Custom Solution Modeler) platform. Pre-existing excel based models are migrated to CSM platform using Rules frame work. To improve business as per current demand, AT&T business services (ABS) has started an initiative to migrate all these Excel based tools/models in to CSM platform.

Model

Model states that it is combination of business rules, inputs and outputs required to derive cost/price etc. For example, ATT has excel based cost models that derives cost of each product based on requirements. To derive cost, requirements are given as input, business rules are applied on inputs and calculated cost is shown in output.
